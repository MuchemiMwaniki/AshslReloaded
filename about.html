About Autoserve & HW Supplies Ltd

Established with a vision to revolutionize the automotive parts and hardware supply industry, Autoserve & HW Supplies Ltd has grown to become a leading name synonymous with quality, reliability, and exceptional customer service.

Our Journey

From our humble beginnings, we've dedicated ourselves to understanding the intricate needs of the automotive sector. We noticed a gap in the market for a supplier that consistently offered genuine parts, robust hardware, and expert advice under one roof. Driven by this insight, Autoserve & HW Supplies Ltd was founded on the principles of integrity, passion for vehicles, and unwavering commitment to our clients.

Over the years, we've built strong relationships with reputable manufacturers and suppliers worldwide, allowing us to curate an extensive inventory that caters to a diverse range of vehicle makes and models. Our growth is a testament to the trust our customers place in us, and we continuously strive to exceed expectations.

Our Mission & Values

Our mission is simple: to empower vehicle owners, mechanics, and businesses with access to the highest quality automotive parts and hardware, ensuring safety, performance, and longevity. We believe in building lasting partnerships through transparency, respect, and mutual growth.

Our core values guide every decision we make:

  • Quality First: We source only genuine and high-standard aftermarket parts, rigorously vetted for durability and performance.
  • Customer Centricity: Your needs are at the heart of our operations. We provide personalized service and expert guidance.
  • Integrity: We conduct our business with honesty, fairness, and a commitment to ethical practices.
  • Innovation: Continuously seeking new and improved solutions in automotive technology and supply chain management.
  • Reliability: We ensure timely delivery and consistent availability of essential components.

Meet Our Expert Team

Behind Autoserve & HW Supplies Ltd is a dedicated team of automotive enthusiasts and supply chain specialists. We are passionate about what we do and are always ready to share our knowledge and help you find the perfect part for your needs.

We look forward to serving you and becoming your preferred partner for all your vehicle parts and hardware needs.
